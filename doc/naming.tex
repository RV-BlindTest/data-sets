\section{Preliminary remarks}

Every data file in the \texttt{data-sets} repository must have extension \textbf{\texttt{.dat}} and be a standard ASCII file with three columns, corresponding to Julian day, radial velocity and uncertainty in radial velocity:

\paragraph{file.dat} ~\\
\vskip1ex
\begin{tabular}[]{ccc}
jdb & vrad & svrad \\
\ldots & \ldots & \ldots \\
\end{tabular}
\vskip2ex
